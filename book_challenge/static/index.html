Book Challenge

Hello super reader! Welcome to Uncle Scott's Super Readers Reading Challenge where you get points for reading everyday!

How can I earn points?

You earn points by reading or being read to. Every page you read is 1 point. Every book you read is 10 points. If you read a page or more a day for a whole week that's 100 points. If you read a page or more a day for a whole month you get 500 points. And finally if you truly are a super reader and read a page or more every day for two months straight all points are doubled. Meaning every page you read is worth 2 points; every book is worth 20 points; a week of reading is 200 points; a month of reading is 1000 points.

What can I do with these points?

You spend these points on various prizes:

  • 100 points -> Slurpee from 7-11
  • 200 points -> Movie Night!
  • 1000 points -> Small toy (Hotwheels car, Beyblade, or something similar)
  • 5000 points -> NEW SWITCH GAME
